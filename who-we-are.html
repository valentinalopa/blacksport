Who We Are

Built by
Operators,
for Operators.

Black Sports was founded by senior event operations professionals who have collectively delivered over 30 major international sporting events. We exist because we understand the gap between strategic planning and operational reality.

Our team has worked at the highest levels of the Olympic movement, FIFA, World Athletics, and major continental games — not as observers, but as the people who made them function.

We are part of the Black Engineering Group, a multidisciplinary infrastructure and engineering consultancy with a global portfolio. This relationship means our sports operations expertise is underpinned by deep technical and engineering capability.
